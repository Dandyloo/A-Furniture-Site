FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your business hours?

We're open Monday to Saturday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. We're closed on Sundays and public holidays.

Do you deliver outside Takoradi?

Yes! We deliver across Ghana including Accra, Kumasi, and other major cities. Delivery fees vary based on location.

Can I customize furniture designs?

Absolutely! We specialize in custom furniture. Share your vision with us and we'll bring it to life.

How long does custom furniture take?

Custom orders typically take 2-4 weeks depending on complexity. We'll provide a specific timeline with your quotation.

Do you offer installation services?

Yes, we provide professional installation services for all furniture purchases at competitive rates.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfers, mobile money, and major credit/debit cards for your convenience.
